YouTube A11y Agent

Hands-free: on a YouTube tab the agent speaks when you first interact. Then press the backtick key (`) and speak — it sends when you pause; press again to interrupt. Arrow keys browse the feed; Alt+Shift+A for an overview. These buttons are a visual fallback.

AI replies (optional, bring your own key)

Your key is stored only in this browser (extension storage) and used only to send your spoken requests — plus page text the tools read, like video titles — to Google's Gemini API. It is never sent to the developer. Google may use free-tier prompts to improve its products. Without a key, AI replies aren't available — but every direct command (play, pause, search, browse, open a number…) always works with no AI at all.
